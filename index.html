Scrolling Through Change: How Major Societal Events Reshape Instagram Language

In this project, we ask: Can a language model detect belief shifts in Instagram captioning before versus after major political events, focusing on the 2016 U.S. election, and does the detection differ between influencer accounts and political figure accounts?
AUTHORS
Maya Patel & Vyshu Telukuntla
AFFILIATION
Columbia University
